Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2024

1. Information We Collect

NaniGuard is designed with privacy in mind. We collect minimal information necessary to provide our service:

  • Device Information: Basic device details for app functionality
  • App Usage: How you use NaniGuard features (stored locally)
  • Settings: Your app preferences and configurations
  • Crash Reports: Anonymous error reports to improve app stability

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide and maintain the NaniGuard service
  • Improve app functionality and user experience
  • Troubleshoot technical issues
  • Send important app updates and security notifications

3. Data Storage and Security

Your privacy is our priority:

  • Local Storage: Most data is stored locally on your device
  • No Personal Data Sharing: We never sell or share personal information
  • Encryption: Sensitive data is encrypted using industry standards
  • Minimal Cloud Data: Only necessary for app functionality

4. Permissions We Request

NaniGuard requires specific permissions to function:

  • Display Over Other Apps: To show protective overlay
  • Notification Access: For quick toggle functionality
  • Quick Settings Access: For notification drawer toggle
  • Device Admin (Optional): For enhanced protection features

All permissions are used solely for app functionality and child protection.

5. Children's Privacy

NaniGuard is designed to protect children:

  • We do not collect personal information from children under 13
  • All data collection is parent/guardian controlled
  • No behavioral tracking or profiling of children
  • Compliance with COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act)

6. Third-Party Services

NaniGuard may use third-party services for:

  • App analytics (anonymized data only)
  • Crash reporting and bug tracking
  • App store distribution

All third-party services are GDPR and COPPA compliant.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Delete your data (right to be forgotten)
  • Withdraw consent at any time
  • Export your data in a portable format

8. Data Retention

We retain your information only as long as necessary to provide our services. When you uninstall NaniGuard, most data is automatically deleted from your device. Any cloud data is deleted within 30 days of account closure.

9. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes through the app or via email. Your continued use of NaniGuard after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
